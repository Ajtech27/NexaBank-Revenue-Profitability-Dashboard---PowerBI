 ### 1. NexaBank Revenue & Profitability Dashboard
**Domain:** Banking & Finance

A comprehensive revenue analytics dashboard for a fictional mid-sized commercial bank tracking $84.2M in annual revenue across product lines, customer segments, and 5 regional branches.

**Key features:**
- DAX measures for Net Interest Margin, Cost-to-Income ratio, and YoY growth
- Revenue mix breakdown by income stream — NII, Fee, FX, and Other
- Monthly trend analysis combining bar chart and line overlay
- Customer segment profitability across Corporate, Retail, SME, and Private Wealth
- Branch performance ranking table with margin analysis
- Interactive slicers for Region, Segment, and Period

**Key insight:** Corporate Banking drove 43% of total revenue at 22% YoY growth while Private Wealth was the only declining segment — flagging a retention risk for management review.

**Tools:** Power BI · DAX · Power Query · Data Modeling
